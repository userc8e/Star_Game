Caitlyn's Star Game

Game Mechanics:

Use WASD or the arrow keys to navigate

Avoid the grey asteroids! Losing all 3 hearts or getting less than -100 points will end the game!

Gain points by collecting the colorful circles (stars) flying around!

The bigger the star, the more points you get! ... But the bigger the asteroid, the more points you lose!


Each star color gives off a different effect:

Red heals one heart if you have less than 3 hearts

Lime makes each star worth double points

Blue lets you pass through asteroids without getting hurt

Pink makes you move 2x faster

Yellow makes whatever effect you may have last longer
